Ayushmann Khurrana (born Nishant Khurrana on 14 September 1984)
is an Indian actor, singer, and television host. Known for his
portrayals of ordinary men often plagued with dysfunction, he
is the recipient of several awards, including a National Film
Award and three Filmfare Awards.Following a brief setback, he
starred in the commercially and critically successful romance
"Dum Laga Ke Haisha" (2015). Khurrana went on to establish himself
with the comedies "Bareilly Ki Barfi" (2017), "Shubh Mangal Saavdhan"
(2017), "Badhaai Ho" (2018), "Dream Girl" (2019), and "Bala" (2019);
the thriller "Andhadhun" (2018); and the crime drama "Article 15"....
