Alka Yagnik is an Indian playback singer.
She is noted in Indian cinema for a career
spanning over three decades. She holds a
record of being the only nominee in the
category of Female Playback Singer, as in
the year 1994 she had three solo nomination
and the fourth one was with Ila Arun.
Born: 20 March 1966 (age 53 years), Kolkata
Spouse: Neeraj Kapoor (m. 1989)
Albums: Tomar Naam Likhe Debo, Golden Hits, MORE
Children: Syesha Kapoor
